Diego Quintal's Top 5 Movies

Here is my current list of my Top 5 favourite movies of all time:

5. Dune

The first time I watched this movie was an experience like no other, I had no clue what was going on. However, I was so intrigued and interested in finding out more about the film's characters, lore etc; that I actually researched everything there is to know about the Dune universe and I immediately fell in love with the fictional world of Dune. This was a feeling no other movie has ever made me feel.


4. Spider-Man: Into The Spider-Verse

This movie is arguably one of the best animated movies of all time. The animation style was unique and truly paved the way for the animated movie genre.


3. Call Me By Your Name

This movie is one of the if not the most iconic movie in Gay Culture. As a gay dude I truly felt this movie and it will always make me shed a tear.


2. The Hunger Games

The amount of love I have for this dystopian franchise is immeasurable. I love every single one of 'The Hunger Games' movies, but the first movie will always have a special place in my heart.


1. Avengers: Infinity War

Infinity War is, in my opinion, the best superhero team up movie of all time. It is jam-packed with incredible action scenes and emotion.
